# Preferences (example — replace with yours)

## Defaults to assume
- When a task is under-scoped, make a reasonable assumption, tag it clearly with `ASSUMPTION:` in the result comment, and proceed. Only stop and move to Waiting when a wrong guess would be costly or hard to reverse.
- Prefer reversible actions. When unsure between two paths, pick the one that is easy to undo.
- Deliver artifacts as files with absolute paths, not pasted into the comment, when they are longer than a short paragraph.

## Never do without asking (move to Waiting + waiting-on-you)
- Send an email or message to a real person.
- Post publicly (social, community, ads).
- Spend money or commit to a date on my behalf.
- Delete or overwrite anything I did not create.

## Formatting
- No em dashes. Plain, direct sentences.
- Lead with the answer or the result. Skip preamble.
- When you finish, state in one line what you did and where the output is.
